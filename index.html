Clarity for the Heart, Powered by Intelligence.

We bring science, emotional intelligence, and technology together to help relationships thrive—with understanding at the core.

At Ataraxia Research, we are redefining how people understand and navigate romantic relationships. Our mission is simple yet transformative: to harness the power of intelligent conversation analysis to bring clarity, empathy, and psychological insight into the complexities of human connection.

What are we doing?

We’re bringing smart intelligence to matters of the heart. The idea is simple: share your conversations, and our assistant makes sense of them, uncovering emotional dynamics, psychological insights, and what’s working or not. Instead of leaving romance to assumptions or miscommunication, we offer clarity so you can understand where things stand, where they’re headed, and what might help. Love is complex, but understanding it doesn’t have to be.

How it helps

By analyzing interactions, the system identifies emotional patterns, communication dynamics, and psychological cues between partners. It then offers thoughtful insights into what might be going wrong, where the relationship is headed, and what can be done to foster deeper understanding and emotional alignment.

Our philosophy

Romance shouldn’t rely on guesswork, miscommunication, or unspoken frustration. With Ataraxia, we bring grounded analysis that centers empathy and clarity—helping you navigate with confidence rather than uncertainty.

Privacy & Care

We are building an assistant that allows users to share their conversations in a secure and private manner. Your data deserves respect; our approach is designed to prioritize confidentiality and psychological safety.

Who are we?

We are a group of PhD students based at King’s College London and the University of Sheffield in the United Kingdom.
